Sudoku Solver & Validator

Solve any Sudoku puzzle or validate your solution

Solution Status

Enter a Sudoku puzzle to get started. Click "Solve Puzzle" to find the solution or "Validate Solution" to check your work.

How to Use

  • Click on any cell to enter a number (1-9)
  • Press "Solve Puzzle" to automatically solve the Sudoku
  • Press "Validate Solution" to check if your solution is correct
  • Use "Clear Board" to reset the puzzle
  • Use "Load Example" to try a sample puzzle

Note: Empty cells should be left blank. The solver will fill them with the correct solution.
